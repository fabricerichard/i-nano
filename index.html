i-nano, a research team

focussed on the measurement methodology of mechanical properties of solids at small scales from load-depth sensing techniques using numerical and experimental investigations

Learn about what we do

Here are our objectives

Extract reliable properties of materials from mechanical testing

Based on identifiability method

Optimize numerical and experimental crossed-analysis

By developping a dedicated software

Link continuum and atomistic modeling of mechanical properties

Based on nanoindentation
